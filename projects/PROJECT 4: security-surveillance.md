# Security Camera System & Backup Strategy

## Equipment
- 2x UniFi G4 Instant camera
- 3x UniFi G5 Bullet cameras (planned upgrade)
- UniFi Protect NVR with 3 dedicated HDDs

## Camera Upgrade Plan
Currently expanding coverage with 3x UniFi G5 Bullet cameras. As part of this upgrade, the existing G4 Instant camera will be relocated to a secondary location rather than retired, extending coverage instead of creating waste. This reflects a practical approach to hardware lifecycle - reusing still-functional equipment where it adds value, rather than always buying entirely new gear.

## Storage Strategy
Recordings are stored locally on 3 dedicated hard drives in the NVR for full-length retention. With additional cameras being added, storage needs will increase - the plan is to expand with more drives rather than relying solely on cloud storage for primary footage.

## Offsite Backup
The last 7 days of recordings are additionally backed up to Google Drive. This provides a disaster-recovery safety net - if local storage is damaged, lost, or compromised, recent footage is still recoverable from an independent location.

## Why This Approach
- **Local-first**: Full retention without relying on internet bandwidth or third-party storage costs
- **Cloud as backup, not primary**: Limited window (7 days) keeps offsite storage costs reasonable while still protecting against local failure
- **Scalable**: Storage and camera coverage can both be expanded incrementally as needs grow
- **Hardware reuse**: Older but functional equipment is redeployed rather than discarded
